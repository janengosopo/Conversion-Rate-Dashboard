# Conversion Rate Dashboard

## 1. Project Overview

**Goal:**  
Provide a centralized view of Conversion Rate (CVR) performance across customer segments, product categories, and regions to help stakeholders track KPIs and identify growth opportunities.

**Tools:**  
Power BI, SQL

**Project Type:**  
Data Cleaning, Data Analysis, Data Visualization

**Process:**  
- Cleaned raw data using SQL  
- Performed exploratory and statistical analysis  
- Built interactive dashboards and visualizations in Power BI

---

## 2. Key Business Questions

This dashboard helps stakeholders understand:

* What is the CVR trend over the last 1–3 years?
* How does monthly actual CVR compare to target CVR?
* Which products have the highest and lowest conversion rates?
* Which price category converts best?
* How do age groups and genders differ in conversion?
* How does CVR vary across markets?

---

## 3. Insights of 2025 Performance

### a. Overall CVR Is Below Target

* Actual CVR: **3.53%** vs Target **4.25%**
* Monthly performance is inconsistent, with stronger conversion in early months and a gradual decline toward year-end.
* Only **6 out of 12 months** exceeded the target.
* **September** showed the strongest performance with a clear spike.

---

### b. Big Gap Between Highest and Lowest Converting Products (~5%)

#### **Top 3 Performing Products**

* **Climbing Rope:** 10.61%
* **Surfboard:** 10.45%
* **Hockey Stick:** 9.09%

#### **Bottom 3 Performing Products**

* **Fitness Tracker:** 5.41%
* **Ice Skates:** 5.41%
* **Dumbbells:** 5.36%

---

### c. Medium and High Price Products Convert Better

* **Medium:** 4.84%
* **High:** 4.57%
* **Low:** 3.32%

---

### d. Younger Customers Convert at Higher Rates

* **Age 26–35:** Highest CVR at **4.50%**
* **≤25 years:** 4.25%
* CVR decreases steadily with older groups
* **66+ years:** Lowest CVR at **2.25%**

---

### e. Performance Varies Across Markets

#### **Top 3 Countries (CVR > 4%)**

* **UK:** 5.19%
* **Italy:** 4.58%
* **Belgium:** 4.27%

#### **Lowest 3 Countries (CVR < 3%)**

* **Spain:** 2.83%
* **France:** 2.70%
* **Germany:** 2.31%
